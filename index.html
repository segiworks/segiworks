Background :

This workshop is designed to provide a creative experience that is not only enjoyable but also beneficial for emotional and mental well-being. Using Art Therapy methods that incorporate painting, participants will be encouraged to channel their positive energy and feelings through activities that enhance balance and creativity. This workshop serves as an outlet for energy, emotional expression, and a creative platform.


Objective :

Participants can express their creative ideas and feelings through painting. Currently, opportunities to explore creativity in ways that support emotional well-being are rarely developed. Therefore, this activity is an appropriate means to reawaken awareness of the importance of balancing energy and creativity in everyday life.


Event description:

This workshop features two engaging creative activities: Painting on Canvas. This activity aims to provide participants with a new experience in creating unique and personalized products, while also supporting local MSMEs in the painting industry. In this workshop, participants will be guided by professional speakers from leading MSMEs in Indonesia. In the Painting on Canvas session, participants will have the opportunity to create on various media such as bucket hats, tote bags, and canvas. This activity will be guided by a team from (@dayofart.id/@artpainting), with two focused methods:

Art Therapy: painting by expressing emotional feelings and pouring them onto canvas. Participants will be accompanied in an intimate and private atmosphere so they can feel safe and comfortable. The event will be hosted by a professional therapist to provide psychological and emotional support, ensuring smooth interaction, known as therapeutic communication, between participants and the presenter.
